Get to know Brac island by our taxi service

Supetar | Island of Brac

Supetar is the biggest town on Brac and it's largest port connected directly with Split. It has beatiful church, big bay with lots of nice beaches and a great promenade with numerous caffe bars and restaurants. There are all the things you will maybe need during your stay on the island: hospital, post office, green market, etc. Supetar is great weather you are spending your whole vacation there or just passing by to other places on Brac!

Bol and Zlatni rat | Island of Brac

For many people Bol is the most beautiful place on Brac island. Beside the most famous beach in Croatia (and maybe in the world!), Zlatni rat, you can enjoy in vast number of restaurants, bistors and bars in this charming little town on the south side of Brac. You can get lost in the narrow streets of this town, somewhere between old stone house - real architectural jewels. Bol is very well connected with city of Split by a catamaran line.

Vidova gora | Island of Brac

If you are passing through the interior of Brac you must take a short trip to visit one of the Brac's most famous natural landmarks. Vidova gora is the highest peak on all of the Adriatic islands with 780 meters above the sea level. From there, you can enjoy a great view on a big portion of Adriatic sea, island of Hvar, famous Zlatni rat beach near Bol and many other landmarks.

Wineries | Island of Brac

Brac island is famous for it's wine, wineries and vineyards. Especially for red wine from the sort plavac mali, whith big vineyards on the southern slopes of the island. Use our taxi's to visit some of the local winerys and vineyards and have the unforgettable wine tasting experience in traditional environment. There are nice family wineries located in Nerezisca nd Dracevica in the inland part of Brac.
